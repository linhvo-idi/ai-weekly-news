Where Should INGOs Start Their Generative AI Journey?

ICTworks ¬∑ Feb 3, 2026

Matt Haikin's conversation with Oxfam Denmark's Program Director captures where most INGOs actually are: still in "Awareness" or early "Experimentation" phases, despite the FOMO. Most organizations lack internal capacity to challenge vendor claims or prepare their fragmented data for AI. The piece identifies five "layers" of AI use: individual productivity, team efficiency, customer-facing applications, process optimization, and strategic decision-making. Four priority actions: invest in data foundations, build internal AI literacy, start with bounded experiments, and create governance frameworks before scaling.

Key Insight

The gap between AI capability and organizational readiness is widening. Decision-making defaults to whoever sounds most confident‚Äîoften external vendors. INGOs need structured approaches to build internal capacity before procurement.

OpenAI Confirms Ads Coming to ChatGPT

SlashGear ¬∑ Feb 4, 2026

Despite Sam Altman calling ads a "last resort" in October 2024, OpenAI confirmed in mid-January 2026 that it will begin testing ads for free and Go-tier ($8/month) users in the US. This arrives alongside the new ChatGPT Go subscription tier, creating a three-tier structure: Free (with ads), Go (limited ads), Plus (no ads). Key Insight

For development organizations: free-tier AI tools may increasingly include advertising or data collection trade-offs. Budget planning for AI adoption should factor in whether ad-free tiers are needed for client-facing or sensitive work.
